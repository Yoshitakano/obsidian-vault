# フランス革命 ― Want you！ 市民のハートに、レボ☆リューション！【COTEN RADIO #47】

## 要約 (NotebookLM AI要約)

This episode of COTEN RADIO introduces the **French Revolution** as a pivotal historical event that laid the foundation for the **modern concept of human rights**, including the **freedom and equality** we often take for granted today. The hosts explain that before 1789, people were largely considered the **property of monarchs**, but this revolution attempted to replace that old regime with a society driven by **idealistic ideologies** rather than mere pragmatic governance. They emphasize the intense **human passion** and youthful energy of the revolutionaries, noting that while their vision was noble, it led to a **chaotic and bloody struggle** that ultimately reshaped global values. By framing the revolution as a high-stakes **social venture**, the speakers illustrate how the movement's successes and messy failures birthed the **fundamental rights** that continue to define contemporary life.
