# 正義の化身！ロベスピエールによる恐怖政治・テルミドール9日のクーデタ―【COTEN RADIO #54】

## 要約 (NotebookLM AI要約)

Following the execution of Louis XVI, the French Revolution entered a volatile phase where the **Robespierre-led Mountain faction** seized power, radicalizing the movement toward an **ideology-based democracy**. This era, defined by the **Reign of Terror**, saw the suspension of a visionary constitution in favor of a **dictatorial revolutionary government** tasked with defending the Republic against a coalition of European monarchies. Robespierre is depicted as a man of **uncompromising integrity and severe moralism** who believed so deeply in his version of justice that he viewed any dissent as a **treacherous conspiracy** punishable by death. Ultimately, this period of "cleansing" the state through the guillotine collapsed when his own colleagues, fearing for their lives, orchestrated the **Thermidorian Reaction** to execute him. The narrative concludes that while the revolution was chaotic and bloody, it planted the **essential seeds of modern human rights** and a universal political consciousness that fundamentally differ from the top-down modernization seen in events like Japan's Meiji Restoration.
